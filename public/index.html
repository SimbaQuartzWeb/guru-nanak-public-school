About Us

TEACHING THE PROFESSIONALS OF TOMORROW

Guru Nanak Public School, Rayya, Amritsar is a prestigious English medium institution known far and wide for its quality education.

Founded by Chairman/ President Dr Gurmeet Singh Cheema in 1986. Within a short timespan our school is enjoying name and fame. We are pleased to have over 1700 students in the campus being taught by around 50 teachers, who consider each child’s success as their goal.

The school premises is spread in about 2 acres of land with modern architechture having 47 spacious rooms.

Professional Faculty

Library
